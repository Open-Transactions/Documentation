Makes an exact copy of a purse, except with no tokens inside. (Value of zero.) For example, if you have a password-protected purse, you might want to pop all of the tokens off of it, and as you iterate, re-assign half of them to new ownership, push those onto a new purse owned by that new owner. But you only want to do this with half of the tokens... the other half of the tokens, you just want to push onto a temp purse (for the loop) that's owned by the original owner, so you can then save it back over the original in storage (since it contains "all the tokens that WEREN'T deposited" or "all the tokens that WEREN'T exported" etc.
