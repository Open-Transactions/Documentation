01464     FAILED, then maybe the opening number is already gone, even though his closing numbers, on the
01465     other hand, are still valid for retrieval. (I have to double check this.)
01466

01468     the opening number is still good also, and should be retrieved.
01469

01471     They will appear on EVERY balance agreement and transaction statement from here until the end
01472     of time, whenever I finally close out those numbers. If some of them are still good on a failed
01473     transaction, then I want to retrieve them so I can use them, and eventually close them out.
01474 
01475     ==> Whereas, what if I am the PARTY to a smart contract, but I am not the actual ACTIVATOR / ORIGINATOR
01476     (who activated the smart contract on the server). Therefore I never sent any transaction to the
01477     server, and I never burned my opening number. It's probably still a good #. If my wallet is not a piece
01478     of shit, then I should have a stored copy of any contract that I signed. If it turns out in the future
01479     that that contract wasn't activated, then I can retrieve not only my closing numbers, but my OPENING
01480     number as well! IN THAT CASE, I would call HarvestAllNumbers() instead of HarvestClosingNumbers().
01481
