01484     This function will load up the cron item (which is either a market offer, a payment plan,
01485     or a SMART CONTRACT.) UPDATE: this function operates on messages, not cron items.

01488     available to be harvested from THE_CRON_ITEM. (There might be zero #s available for that
01489     Nym, which is still a success and will return true. False means error.)
01490

01492 
01493     WHY WOULD I WANT to harvest ONLY the closing numbers for the Nym, and not the OPENING
01494     numbers as well? The answer is because for this Nym, the opening number might already
01495     be burned. For example, if Nym just tried to activate a smart contract, and the activation
01496     FAILED, then maybe the opening number is already gone, even though his closing numbers, on the
01497     other hand, are still valid for retrieval. (I have to double check this.)
01498

01500     the opening number is still good also, and should be retrieved.
01501

01503     They will appear on EVERY balance agreement and transaction statement from here until the end
01504     of time, whenever I finally close out those numbers. If some of them are still good on a failed
01505     transaction, then I want to retrieve them so I can use them, and eventually close them out.
01506 
01507     ==> Whereas, what if I am the PARTY to a smart contract, but I am not the actual ACTIVATOR / ORIGINATOR
01508     (who activated the smart contract on the server). Therefore I never sent any transaction to the
01509     server, and I never burned my opening number. It's probably still a good #. If my wallet is not a piece
01510     of shit, then I should have a stored copy of any contract that I signed. If it turns out in the future
01511     that that contract wasn't activated, then I can retrieve not only my closing numbers, but my OPENING
01512     number as well! IN THAT CASE, I would call HarvestAllNumbers() instead of HarvestClosingNumbers().
01513
