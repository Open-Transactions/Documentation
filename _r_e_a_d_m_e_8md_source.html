00005 The Open-Transactions project is a collaborative effort to develop
00006 a robust, commercial-grade, fully-featured, free-software toolkit
00007 implementing the OTX protocol as well as a full-strength financial
00008 cryptography library, API, CLI, and prototype server. The project
00009 is managed by a worldwide community of volunteers that use the

00011 toolkit and its related documentation.

00017 ### About
00018 
00019 Open-Transactions democratizes financial and monetary actions. You
00020 can use it for issuing currencies/stock, paying dividends, creating
00021 asset accounts, sending/receiving digital cash, writing/depositing
00022 cheques, cashier's cheques, creating basket currencies, trading on
00023 markets, scripting custom agreements, recurring payments, escrow,
00024 etc.
00025 
00026 Open-Transactions uses strong crypto. The balances are unchangeable
00027 (even by a malicious server.) The receipts are destructible and
00028 redundant. The transactions are unforgeable. The cash is untraceable.
00029 The cheques are non-repudiable. Etc.
00030 
00031 This product includes software developed by Ben Laurie for use in
